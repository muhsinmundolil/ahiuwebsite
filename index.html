Academic Offerings

College of Islamic Sharia

An eight-year course in Islamic Studies comprising many disciplines in Islamic Sciences, including Quran Interpretation(Thafseer), Islamic Jurisprudence, Hadith, Arabic Language & Literature, Arabic Grammar & Morphology, Islamic Theology & Philosophy, Logic, Foundations of Hadith and Fiqh, Aqeeda and Islamic History. This course also includes school education from 8th grade to Undergraduate Degree courses.

College for Quran Memorization

Course for memorizing the Holy Quran with its rules (tajweed) in the qira’ath (recitation) of Hafs from Aasim, which takes a maximum of three years. The students, then, shall be eligible for admission to both the colleges of study, i.e. College of Islamic Sharia and College of Qur'an Studies.

College of Quran Studies

A course equivalent to Islamic Sharia, which focuses more on Quranic subjects and those who can enrol in this course should be a Hafiz (memorized the Holy Quran )
